---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project delivers a comprehensive financial comparison of Pakistan's leading corporations using **Microsoft Excel**. The dashboard presents clean, visual insights into revenue performance and market valuation — designed to support data-driven decision making and financial literacy.

This project was also presented as a **research poster at FICPFSIT-2026**, an international conference on financial and computational sciences.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- **KPI Cards** — Total & average revenue and market cap at a glance
- **Revenue Bar Chart** — Horizontal comparison of all 6 companies by revenue
- **Market Cap Bar Chart** — Visual ranking by market capitalization (USD Bn)
- **Revenue Share Pie Chart** — PSO dominant share clearly visible
- **Data Table** — Company-wise breakdown with notes column
- **Insights Panel** — 4 written analytical conclusions

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- 🥇 **PSO dominates revenue** — PKR 3.74 trillion, proving the energy sector drives Pakistan's economy
- 💰 **UBL leads market cap** — ~$4.0 billion despite lower revenue than PSO, showing investors highly value banking sector companies
- 🏗️ **Lucky Cement** — smaller revenue (PKR 411 Bn) but strong market cap ($2.54 Bn), proving cement companies remain highly profitable
- 📈 **OGDCL & HBL** — important players with moderate revenue and market cap, showing steady growth and market stability
